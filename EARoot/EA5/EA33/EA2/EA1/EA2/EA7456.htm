The LC_HerbaceousGrowthForms metaobject is a subtype of LC_GrowthForm. It has two subtypes LC_Gramineae and LC_Forbs. This metaobject, when instantiated, allows the generation of a general land cover feature class in an LCCS for all vegetation covered surfaces where the cover is a herbaceous growth form <br/><br/>
