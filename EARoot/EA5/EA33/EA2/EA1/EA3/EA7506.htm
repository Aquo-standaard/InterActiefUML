The LC_BuiltUpSurface metaobject is a subtype of LC_ArtificialSurfaceElement. This metaobject, when instantiated, allows the generation of a general land cover feature class in an LCCS for all built up artificial surfaces. The LC_BuiltUpSurface metaobject has two subtypes LC_LinearSurface and LC_NonLinearSurface.<br/><br/>
