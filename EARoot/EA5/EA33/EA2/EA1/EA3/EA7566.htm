The LC_WaterBodyAndAssociatedSurfaceElement metaobject is a subtype of LC_AbioticElement. This metaobject, when instantiated, allows the generation of a general land cover feature class in an LCCS for all water body and associated surfaces..<br/><br/>It has three subtypes LC_WaterBody, LC_Snow, and LC_Ice and a relationship to two components LC_WaterAndAssociatedSurfacesCharacteristic, and LC_SeasonalVariation.<br/><br/>There is one attribute <i>height</i>, The permitted values of <i>height</i> are defined by the value class LC_PermittedRealValue.<br/><br/>
