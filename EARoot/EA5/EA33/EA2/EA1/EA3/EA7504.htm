The LC_WaterBodyAndAssociatedSurfaceElement metaobject is a subtype of LC_AbioticElement. It has three subtypes LC_WaterBody, LC_Snow, and LC_Ice and a relationship to two components LC_WaterAndAssociatedSurfaceCharacteristic and LC_SeasonalVariation. There is one attribute <i>height</i>. The permitted values of <i>height</i> are defined by the value class LC_PermittedRealValue. <br/><br/>
