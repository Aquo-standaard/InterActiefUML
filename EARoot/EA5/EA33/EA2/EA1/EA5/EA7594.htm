The LC_WaterAndAssociatedSurfaceCharacteristic component is a subtype of LC_LandCoverElementCharacteristic. The LC_WaterAndAssociatedSurfacesCharacteristic metaobject is a component related to the LC_WaterBodyAndAssociatedSurfaceElement metaobject by the relation <i>waterBodyAndAssociatedSurfaceCharacteristic</i>. It has five subtypes LC_Aquaculture, LC_Artificiality, LC_WaterSalinity, LC_SnowCategory and LC_IceCategory. The component LC_WaterAndAssociatedSurfacesCharacteristic may be used to refine the metaobject LC_WaterBodyAndAssociatedSurfaceElement to permit the generation of a more specific land cover feature class in an LCCS.<br/><br/>
