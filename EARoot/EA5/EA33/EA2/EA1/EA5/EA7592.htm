The LC_ArtificialSurfaceCharacteristic component is a subtype of LC_LandCoverElementCharacteristic. The component LC_ArtificialSurfaceCharacteristic is related to the LC_ArtificialSurfaceElement metaobject by the relation <i>artificialSurfaceCharacteristic</i>. It has two subtypes LC_ConstructionStatus and LC_ConstructionUse. The component LC_ArtificialSurfaceCharacteristic may be used to refine the metaobject LC_ArtificialSurfaceElement to permit the generation of a more specific land cover feature class in an LCCS. <br/><br/>
